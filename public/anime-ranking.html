My Top 5 Anime of all-time

Guilty Crown

This anime is fire. From the stunning visuals to the amazing OST soundtrack.

Bocchi The Rock!

Show is amazing and really represents what it's like to be an introvert.

Angel Beats!

Anime that hits the feels. From dying from laughter to crying along with the characters, there's really nothing else to say about it.

Code Geass

Makes you wonder what happens when you obtain the power to rule over everyone.

Violet Evergarden

Outstanding visuals with a large dose of feels.
